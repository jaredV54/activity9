My Favorite Food Delicacies

Good food is a source of comfort and joy. Here are 5 of my favorite foods and cuisines:
Adobo
Adobo is a classic Filipino dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
Pininyahang Manok
Pininyahang Manok This dish literally translates to pineapple chicken. This Recipe calls for chicken slices to be marinated in pineapple juice then stewed with pineapple chunks.
Sinigang
Sinigang is a sour soup made with pork, shrimp, or fish, flavored with tamarind and assorted vegetables.
Fried Chicken
Fried Chicken is crispy and juicy chicken pieces deep-fried to golden perfection, a universal comfort food.
Kare-Kare
Kare-Kare is a traditional Filipino stew with oxtail, vegetables, and a rich peanut sauce, often served with bagoong (shrimp paste).
